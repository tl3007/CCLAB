CAPUCCINO INGREDIENTS:

1/4 cup cold milk
1 rounded tbsp finely ground coffee, preferably
an expresso blend
1 pinch cocoa powder or ground cinnamon,
optional

STEPS:

#1: Steam the milk, then let the milk simmer
until bubbles form around the edges.
#2: Next, whip the milk with an electric mixer. Continue mixing until you get the desired volume
of froth.
#3: Now combine the ingredients! A classic capuccino calss for 1/3 espresso, 1/3 steamed
milk, and 1/3 foam.

TOTAL: 10 mins

CARAMEL LATTE INGREDIENTS:

1/2 cup milk
1 tbsp brown sugar
1 tbsp sugar-free caramel topping
1 tbsp caramel sauce
1/4 tbsp vanilla extract
1 cup coffee

STEPS:

#1: Combine milk and brown sugar in a
saucepan over medium heat.
#2: Steam the milk, then let the milk simmer
until bubbles form around the edges. Do not boil
the milk!
#3: Reduce heat to low; whisk in caramel sauce
and vanilla extract. Mix in coffee.

TOTAL: 8 mins

MOCHA INGREDIENTS:

2 tbsp milk
1 tbsp white sugar
1 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
1 cup hot brewed coffee

STEPS:

#1: In a small pot or microwave-safe cup, stir together sugar, cocoa powder, and water.
Heat on the stove or in your microwave, and stir until sauce is completely smooth.
#2: Add coffee and milk and continue to heat
until steaming. If making on the stove, you can whisk the mixture vigorously until foamy.
Pour into a cup, top with whipped cream if desired, and enjoy!

TOTAL: 10 mins
