Caramel Latte

INGREDIENTS:

½ cup milk
1 tablespoon brown sugar
1 tablespoon sugar-free caramel topping
1 tablespoon caramel sauce
¼ teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup coffee


STEPS:

#1: Combine milk and brown sugar in a
saucepan over medium heat.
#2: Heat until frothy and close to simmering,
about 3 minutes.
#3: Reduce heat to low; whisk in caramel sauce
and vanilla extract.Mix in coffee.

TOTAL: 8 mins
